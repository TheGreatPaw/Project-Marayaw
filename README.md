# Marayaw
A digital gateway and mobile application designed to promote local tourism in **Lanao del Sur** and preserve the rich **Maranao culture**.

---

## Overview
Derived from the Maranao language, **"Marayaw"** translates to **"beautiful"** or **"splendid"**. The application serves as an all-in-one platform for tourists and locals to discover attractions, historical sites, cultural events, and recommendations for food and lodging. By integrating AI-driven suggestions and cultural preservation efforts, Marayaw aims to boost the local economy while providing a modern travel experience.

## Problem
Currently, Lanao del Sur faces several challenges in the tourism sector:
* **Lack of Centralization:** There is no single platform providing complete and updated information regarding local attractions and services.
* **Planning Difficulties:** Tourists struggle to discover hidden gems due to a lack of reliable sources.
* **User Experience Gaps:** The absence of digital tools like interactive maps, itineraries, and translation services reduces travel convenience.
* **Heritage Preservation:** There is a critical need for a modern platform to highlight and promote Maranao heritage.

## Sustainable Development Goal (SDG Alignment)
Marayaw is committed to the following United Nations Sustainable Development Goals:
* **SDG 8:** Decent Work and Economic Growth.
* **SDG 17:** Partnerships for the Goals.

## Solutions
* **All-in-One Tourism App:** A one-stop platform featuring cultural content, history, and local service listings.
* **Smart Travel Tools:** Integration of interactive maps, AI-based itinerary planning, geo-tagging, and AR guides.
* **Personalized Experience:** AI-powered recommendations and built-in translation services for Maranao, Filipino, and English.
* **Local Partnerships & Safety:** Collaboration with local businesses for exclusive deals and the inclusion of emergency hotlines and safety alerts.

## Proposed Use of Funds
The project budget is allocated as follows to ensure growth and sustainability:
* **30%** - Application Development.
* **25%** - Marketing & Promotion.
* **20%** - Content Creation & Translation Services.
* **15%** - Maintenance & Updates.
* **10%** - Community & Sustainability Initiatives.

## Features
1. **Explore & Discover:** Find top attractions and hidden gems with user reviews and personalized recommendations.
2. **Interactive Maps & Navigation:** Navigate Lanao del Sur efficiently with real-time maps and geo-tagged points of interest.
3. **Trip Planner:** Organize itineraries, add destinations, and stay updated on local festivals and events.
4. **Bookings & Payments:** Securely book tours, accommodations, and tickets with direct in-app payments.
5. **Traveler Support:** Access multi-language translation tools and emergency assistance for a safer journey.

## Progress/Traction
* **Months 1–3:** Launch campaign targeting **2,000 downloads**; initial seed funding of ₱200,000 for development and promotion.
* **Months 4–6:** Onboard **20+ local businesses** (restaurants, hotels, guides) and reach **5,000 active users**.
* **Months 7–12:** Full integration of booking features, reaching **10,000 downloads**, **50+ business partners**, and a revenue target of **₱500,000**.
